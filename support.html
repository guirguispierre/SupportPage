Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I explore random articles?

    Open the app, and you'll see random Wikipedia articles displayed. Scroll through them or tap 'Load More' to see new articles.

  • How do I bookmark an article?

    Tap the bookmark icon on the article card to save it. You can view all your bookmarks in the Bookmarks tab.

  • How do I change the font size?

    Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner of the Home screen to open Settings, then use the slider to adjust the font size.
